My name is Elliot Varella and I'm thrilled to be the tennis captain here at the multi sports complex. I've gained a lot of experience both on and off the court, and I'm excited to bring that experience to my role as captain. For me, being a captain is all about leadership. I strive to be a positive influence on and off the court, and I always try to lead by example. I believe that my positive attitude and willingness to help others has earned me the respect of my peers and opponents. I'm always looking for ways to motivate and inspire my teammates, and I'm dedicated to helping them improve their game. As captain, my responsibilities include working closely with our coach to develop practice plans and match strategies, and communicating these plans to my teammates. I also organize team meetings and ensure that everyone is aware of the schedule for matches and tournaments. During matches, I provide guidance and support to my teammates, and I'm always looking for ways to improve their performance. Overall, I feel honoured to be the tennis captain at MSL. As the captain, I'm responsible for managing the tennis program at the club, from coaching to organising tournaments. My goal as a coach and captain is to instill these values in my players and help them become not only better athletes, but also better individuals. Being the tennis captain at the local sports complex is an honor and a privilege. I love being able to contribute to the growth of the club and the sport I'm so passionate about. Club Rules

Using the courts is FREE to all members but all courts must still be booked online by clicking here. Club events, tournaments and competitions and coaching are all pre-booked. Hard courts (Nos 9 & 10) are avilable throughout the year and ten grass courts (Nos 1 & 8) weather permitting May to September.

Friends and Family of memebers are always welcome at £5 per session per Adult and £3 per session as junior (max 5 pe year) payable in shop or to any Committee Member, or staff member.

Non Memebers are welcome to book and play on the day. Court Sessions last 1/5 hours and cost £20 for 4 adults, £16 for familt of 2 Adults and 2 Children, £12 for 2 players and £3 each for juniors. Please book and pay at the shop on arrival.

Every Tuesdays and Thursday mornings all year 10.00am - 12.00pm (mid-July to end August 14.00 - 16:00pm). Wednesday evenings May to September from 5.00pm. all members and visitors are welcome.

Recognised tennis shoes and appropriate clothing is expected to be worn on the Courts. Please use tennis changing rooms located to the right of the courts.

A comprehensive programme of events is organised by the Tennis Committee. Notices will be posted on the tennis section notice board along with entry lists and other details.

Junior, Men's, Ladies and mixed doubles tournaments are held throughout July and August. mini/tennis coaching ages (5- 11) sessions are held every morning.

Individual and group coaching is available for adults and juniors please contact De-ann Stewart-Leigh For general advice and furthur details please ocntact the shop

MSL Tennis is committed to prioritising the well-being of all children and adults, promoting safeguarding in our club at all times, including all programmes and events that we run. In addition we are proud to have a Diversity and Inclusion Policy that demonstrates our commitment to making tennis diverse and inclusive for all. We are committed to ensuring our practices reflect statutory responsibilities, government guidance and complies with best practice and LTA requirements. A copy of the club’s full Safeguarding Policy can be read here The Diversity and Inclusion Policy can be read here. The Whistleblowing Policy can be read here. All policies are also displayed in the tennis pavilion, and are also available from the club office and the MSL Welfare Officer
