About MSL and what we offer


I created this website, to help sport enthusiasts like myself keep track of their leagues. I wanted to do this in a simple manner where everything is easy to use and where you can get all the information you need from your league, competition details, venues and more.


At MSL, we believe that sports are an integral part of building healthy communities and promoting a healthy lifestyle. Our league is open to individuals of all ages and skill levels, from beginners to seasoned athletes. We offer a range of divisions, so you can find the one that best suits your level of play.

Our league is run by a dedicated team of organizers who are passionate about sports and committed to creating a fun and inclusive environment for all participants. We strive to provide a positive experience for our players, with fair and consistent rules, and a focus on good sportsmanship.

So, whether you're looking to compete at a high level, stay active, or simply meet new people who share your love of sports, MSL has something for everyone. Join us today and experience the excitement of multiple sports in a fun and supportive environment!
